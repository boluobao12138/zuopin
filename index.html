About / 关于

菠萝包
要压扁吃

Brand Designer / 品牌设计师

旅居海外的品牌设计师，相信好的设计不是装饰，而是让品牌找到属于自己的声音。作品横跨品牌识别、包装、编辑与动态影像，服务于时尚、美妆、生活方式及文化领域客户。

Based between London and Shanghai. I craft brand identities that resonate across cultures — where every detail carries intention and every space tells a story. My practice sits at the intersection of strategic clarity and visual poetry.
